Cherry Audio¶

Cherry Audio was formed by a small group of industry veterans from the likes of Sonic Foundry, Cakewalk, Bias, Acoustica, and Keyboard Magazine

  • Mercury-6 - meticulous recreation of the Jupiter-6 analog synthesizer
  • PS-3300 - an exactingly faithful virtual analog emulation of the colossal and ultra-rare semi-modular analog polyphonic synthesizer released by KORG in 1977
  • Synthesizer Expander Module Synthesizer - emulation of the classic Oberheim® self‑contained synthesizer module
