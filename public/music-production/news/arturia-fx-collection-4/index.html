Overview¶

[Arturia] have just released FX Collection 4 which now includes 30 effects.

There is currently an intro offer, you can get 40% off until 20th June 2023

  • List price: £429
  • Discounted price: £257
  • Note: If you’re an owner of other Arturia products you will likely get an even bigger discount

The following effects have been added since collection 3;

  • Rotary CLS-222 - reimagines the classic Dynacord® rotary speaker emulator as a creative plugin that goes from silky smooth stereo swirls to spinning synchronized patterns - enhancing your sound with extra width, momentum, and analog character in seconds
  • Dist COLDFIRE - dual-engine distortion effect that goes from rich tube saturation to unrestrained sonic destruction in a few clicks
  • Filter MS-20 - stereo filter and distortion effect modeled on the infamously abrasive MS-20 synthesizer
  • Rev LX-24 - an enhanced virtual replica of a retro ambience box that defined reverb for a generation
