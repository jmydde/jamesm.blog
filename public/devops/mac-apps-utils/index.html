Mac Utilities¶

A list of recommended Mac utilities …

  • Alfred - award-winning app for macOS which boosts your efficiency with hotkeys, keywords, text expansion & more
  • Amphetamine - powerful keep-awake utility
  • CleanMyMac - cleanup utility for macOS
  • CleanShot - capture your Mac’s screen like a pro
  • CloudMounter - mount your Dropbox, Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Amazon S3, etc.
  • DirEqual - compare folders
  • DiskCatalogMaker - catalog all your discs
  • Disk Space Analyzer Pro - analyze & cleanup disk space
  • Easy Data Transform - merge, split, clean, de-duplicate & much more, without coding
  • FileZilla - FTP, FTPS & SFTP client
  • GitKraken - powerful suite of Git tools
  • Google Drive - cloud storage
  • iTerm2 - terminal emulator
  • Kaleidoscope - spot the differences in text & image files, or even folders full of files
  • LastPass - password manager
  • Loom - screen recorder
  • NordPVN - VPN
  • OmniPlayer Pro - play or cast video audio media
  • Otter - transcribe voice to text
  • Path Finder - all the tools you need to take control of your files
  • Pure Paste - paste as plain text by default
  • TextSoap - powerful text transformations with just a click, stop doing this stuff by hand
  • Text Workflow - combine transformation actions & create powerful automations for your text
  • The Unarchiver - open any type of archive file
  • VisualDiffer - compare folders & files
  • VLC - free & open source cross-platform multimedia player & framework that plays most multimedia files as well as DVDs, Audio CDs, VCDs, and various streaming protocols
