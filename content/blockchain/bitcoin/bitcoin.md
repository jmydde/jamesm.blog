## Overview

Bitcoin is the world's first and largest cryptocurrency by market capitalization, launched in January 2009. Unlike traditional fiat currency issued by central banks, Bitcoin is created, distributed, traded, and stored using a decentralized peer-to-peer network secured by a distributed ledger technology known as blockchain.

### Key Characteristics

- **Decentralized**  -  No central authority or government controls Bitcoin
- **Limited Supply**  -  Maximum of 21 million bitcoins will ever exist
- **Transparent**  -  All transactions are recorded on the public blockchain
- **Immutable**  -  Past transactions cannot be altered or reversed
- **Secure**  -  Protected by cryptographic proof-of-work consensus mechanism
