## About

Dr. Gavin James Wood is an influential English computer scientist and entrepreneur who has fundamentally shaped modern blockchain architecture. As a co-founder of Ethereum, he contributed to the foundational concepts of smart contracts and the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM). Subsequently, he founded Polkadot and Kusama, designing next-generation blockchain ecosystems focused on interoperability and scalability.
