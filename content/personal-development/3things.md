## How to Use This Practice

This is a simple evening reflection exercise designed to cultivate gratitude, build intentional habits, and train your mind to notice the positive. Spend 5 - 10 minutes at the end of each day answering these questions in a journal or notes app.

## The Four Questions

- **What 3 things am I grateful for today?** - Identify moments, people, or experiences you appreciated
- **What 3 things could I do tomorrow that would make it a great day?** - Set intentional goals for the next day
- **An affirmation** - Complete the statement: "I am…" with something meaningful to you
- **What 3 amazing things happened today?** - Reflect on small wins and positive moments you may have overlooked

## Why This Works

This practice rewires your brain to notice abundance rather than scarcity. Over time, it helps you:
- Develop a more optimistic perspective
- Become more intentional about your days
- Build self-awareness and emotional resilience
- Create a personal record of growth and gratitude
