## Modern Data Stack & Engineering
* [Start Data Engineering](https://www.startdataengineering.com/) - Practical guides and projects for building data platforms.
* [Seattle Data Guy](https://www.seattledataguy.com/) - Insights into the business and technical aspects of data engineering.
* [Airbyte Blog](https://airbyte.com/blog) - Focused on data integration, ELT, and the future of data movement.
* [Databricks Blog](https://www.databricks.com/blog) - Deep dives into Spark, Delta Lake, and Lakehouse architectures.
* [LakeFS Blog](https://lakefs.io/blog/) - Focus on data version control and best practices for data lakes.
* [The Data Engineering Podcast](https://www.dataengineeringpodcast.com/) - Comprehensive discussions on the tools and techniques of data engineering.
* [Eclectic Data](https://www.blefari.com/) - Technical deep dives into data infrastructure and distributed systems.
* [A16Z Data & AI](https://a16z.com/category/data-ai/) - High-level perspectives on the evolution of the data industry.
* [Benn Stancil's Blog](https://benn.substack.com/) - Thought-provoking commentary on the analytics and data engineering world.
