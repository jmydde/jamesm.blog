### For Cloud Certifications
When choosing between cloud platforms:
- **AWS**: Largest market share, broadest opportunities, AWS DEA-C01 has best cost-to-value ratio ($150, 3-year validity)
- **GCP**: Strongest in data analytics & ML; Professional Data Engineer averaging $129K-$172K salary; most technically rigorous
- **Azure**: Microsoft Fabric Data Engineer Associate (DP-700) recently launched; offers exceptional value at $165 with free renewals

**Recommendation for Data Engineers (2026):** Start with GCP or AWS based on your specialization (GCP for BigQuery/Vertex AI, AWS for SageMaker/Redshift), then add 1-2 specializations (Databricks, dbt, Terraform)
