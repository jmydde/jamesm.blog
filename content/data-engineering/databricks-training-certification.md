Databricks offers several certification tracks for data engineers, data analysts, ML engineers, and generative AI engineers at both associate and professional levels. Choose based on your role and experience level. All certifications are valid for 2 years and cost $200 per exam attempt.

## Official Databricks Resources

Start here for authoritative training materials and exam information:

- [Databricks Training & Certification](https://www.databricks.com/learn/training/home) - Official certification hub
- [Databricks Certification](https://www.databricks.com/learn/training/certification) - Certification details and exam scheduling
- [Databricks Learning Library](https://www.databricks.com/training/catalog) - Full course catalog
- [Databricks Learn](https://www.databricks.com/learn) - Free learning resources and documentation

### Free Official Courses

- [Lakehouse Platform Fundamentals](https://www.databricks.com/learn/certification/lakehouse-platform-fundamentals) - Free foundational course with accreditation (4 video tutorials + knowledge test)
- [Databricks Fundamentals](https://www.databricks.com/training/catalog/databricks-fundamentals-2206) - Core platform concepts

## Certification Tracks

### Data Engineer
Build and optimize data pipelines on the Lakehouse platform. Recent updates (July 2025) now emphasize DLT, Unity Catalog, Delta Sharing, Lakehouse Federation, and Auto Loader.

- **Associate** - Entry-level, covers core Databricks platform and PySpark fundamentals
  - [Udemy: Data Engineer Associate](https://www.udemy.com/course/databricks-certified-data-engineer-associate/)
  - [Whizlabs Practice Test](https://www.whizlabs.com/databricks-certified-data-engineer-associate/)
- **Professional** - Advanced, requires production experience
  - [Udemy: Data Engineer Professional](https://www.udemy.com/course/databricks-certified-data-engineer-professional/)
  - [Whizlabs Practice Test](https://www.whizlabs.com/databricks-certified-data-engineer-professional/)

### Apache Spark Developer
Focuses on PySpark programming and optimization.

- **Associate** - Core Apache Spark with Python
  - [Whizlabs Practice Test](https://www.whizlabs.com/databricks-certified-associate-developer-apache-spark/)

### Data Analyst
SQL-focused analytics on the Lakehouse. Updated (Sept 2025) with emphasis on Unity Catalog and platform-wide skills.

- **Associate** - SQL, analytics, and dashboarding fundamentals
  - [Whizlabs Practice Test](https://www.whizlabs.com/databricks-certified-data-analyst-associate/)

### Machine Learning
Build and deploy ML models on Databricks.

- **Associate** - ML fundamentals and MLflow
  - [Whizlabs Practice Test](https://www.whizlabs.com/databricks-certified-machine-learning-associate/)
- **Professional** - Advanced ML engineering and production deployment
  - [Whizlabs Practice Test](https://www.whizlabs.com/databricks-certified-machine-learning-professional-certification/)

### Generative AI Engineer
Design, build, and deploy Generative AI solutions with Databricks. Latest track launched in 2025.

- **Associate** - GenAI fundamentals on Databricks platform
