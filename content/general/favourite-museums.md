## Computing

### Bletchley Park
[Bletchley Park](https://bletchleypark.org.uk/) - Historic computer and cryptography museum in the UK

## Science

### Natural History Museum
[Natural History Museum](https://www.nhm.ac.uk/) - World-renowned museum of natural history in London, UK. Home to dinosaur fossils, earth sciences and biology collections.

### British Museum
[British Museum](https://www.britishmuseum.org/) - London's world famous museum of human history, art and culture. One of the largest and most comprehensive collections in existence, covering over two million years of human history.

### Victoria and Albert Museum
[Victoria and Albert Museum](https://www.vam.ac.uk/) - World's largest museum of decorative arts and design, housing over 2.3 million objects spanning 5,000 years of human creativity.

### Imperial War Museum
[Imperial War Museum](https://www.iwm.org.uk/) - Museum documenting the experience of modern warfare and conflict, with exhibits covering from the First World War to the present day.

### Science Museum
[Science Museum](https://www.sciencemuseum.org.uk/) - London's national museum of science, technology, engineering and mathematics. Features historic aircraft, space exploration exhibits and computing history.

## Art Galleries

### National Gallery
[National Gallery](https://www.nationalgallery.org.uk/) - London's premier public art gallery located in Trafalgar Square. Houses one of the greatest collections of European paintings in the world, from the 13th to the 19th centuries.
