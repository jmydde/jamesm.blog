## About
[The Hindley Street Country Club](https://www.thehscc.com/) (HSCC) is an Australian group founded in 2017 by arranger and producer Constantine Delo. The band specializes in innovative cover arrangements that have achieved massive viral success through social media.

Starting as a group of Adelaide musicians recording re-arranged covers, HSCC uploads fresh videos weekly. What sets them apart is their creative approach to arranging classic funk, soul, and R&B tracks with unique instrumental combinations and energetic performances. The formula has resonated globally, earning them over 540 million YouTube views, 940,000+ YouTube subscribers, and more than a billion views across all social media platforms.

Their viral success has transformed them from local musicians into touring artists who perform worldwide while maintaining their commitment to weekly [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@theHindleyStreetCountryClub) releases every Friday. Each video showcases their talented ensemble performing elaborate arrangements that reimagine beloved songs from various genres.
